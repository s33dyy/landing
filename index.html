FAQ

Questions we get every time.

How long does it take to go live?

Most systems are fully live within 14 days. We handle everything — setup, training, integrations, testing. You review and approve.

Do I need any technical knowledge?

None. You tell us how your business works. We build the system. You get a dashboard you can understand without a manual.

What if leads ask something the AI can't answer?

The system is smart enough to hand off to a human when needed. You get notified instantly, and the conversation is right there in your dashboard.

Is this just a chatbot?

No. A chatbot answers questions. Our system captures, qualifies, follows up, books, and tracks — all automatically. It's an operating system for your front desk.

What channels does it work on?

WhatsApp Business, website live chat, inbound calls (AI voice), Instagram DMs, Facebook Messenger, and email — depending on your plan.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No long-term lock-in. We operate on monthly retainers after the build fee. If you want to exit, we make it clean and simple.
